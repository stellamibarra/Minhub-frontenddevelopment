Mision

To support young atlhetes living Chicago's northside neighborhood who have an interest in learning and playing soccer ,with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to the game of hockey,specifically those skills around team cooperation and good sportsmanship

Vision

The Mighty Ducks Hockey League aspires to develop strong,well-rounded and mindful atlhetes through the building of character,self-dicipline,and leadership

General Information

The Mighty Ducks Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide atlhetes residing Chicago's northside neighborhoods an enviroment in which to learn and play hockey.To be a member of MDHL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside a Chicago northside neighborhood MDLH ran by a small full-time staff ,and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members
